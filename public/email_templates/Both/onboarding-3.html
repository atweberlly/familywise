Here's another tip which comes directly from our storytellers. To make the most out of their memberships, our members tell us they're most successful when they get into a routine for their weekly entries.

Some look forward to receiving their weekly email & set time aside to write straight away.

Others find writing at night a nice way to end the day, so they take a few hours to think about how they'll approach their question & sit down after dinner when it's nice & quiet.

Plenty of our community of storytellers told us they like to write early, have a cup of tea nearby & spend some time in the morning before their day gets busy.

It doesn't matter what works for you, what matters is, try not to get too far behind in your weekly sessions, otherwise it might be tricky to catch up.
